🎤🔊 To Use Voice Features: Open in New Tab!

The microphone and speaker won't work in the preview window. Click the button below to open the app in a new tab where all voice features will work perfectly!

🔊 How to Enable the Speaker (Hear the Chatbot)

Step 1: Click the "🚀 Open App in New Tab" button above

Step 2: In the new tab, click anywhere on the page (the background, a button, etc.)

Step 3: Make sure your device volume is turned up 🔊

Step 4: Choose a unit from the "📚 Choose Your Conversation Unit" section and you'll hear the chatbot speak!

💡 If you still can't hear: Check if your browser has sound enabled in the address bar. Look for a 🔇 mute icon and click it to unmute.

🎤 Microphone Instructions:

When you click "🎤 Speak", your browser will ask for permission to use your microphone. Please click "Allow" to enable voice input. If you accidentally blocked it, click the 🔒 lock icon in the address bar and change microphone to "Allow".

🔊 Speaker Instructions (To Hear the Chatbot):

To hear the chatbot speak: 1) Open the app in a new tab (red button above), 2) Click anywhere on the page once, 3) Turn up your volume 🔊, 4) Choose a unit above and the chatbot will speak questions and feedback out loud.
