## 🚀 How It Works

### For Students:
1. **Click a Unit Button** (1-10) on the web page
2. **Bot asks questions** from the selected unit's dialogue
3. **Student answers** (type or speak with microphone)
4. **Bot compares** answer with expected response
5. **Bot gives encouraging feedback** (gentle, positive)
6. **Bot asks extra questions** at the end
7. **Bot celebrates** completion with positive message

### Features:
- ✅ 10 clickable unit buttons
- ✅ American English voice output
- ✅ Microphone input support
- ✅ Encouraging feedback (not harsh)
- ✅ Answer comparison with hints
- ✅ Extra questions after main dialogue
- ✅ Positive reinforcement throughout
